Pepsi is a popular carbonated soft drink produced by PepsiCo. It was created by Caleb Bradham in 1893 and initially named "Brad's Drink" before being renamed Pepsi-Cola in 1898. Known for its distinctive flavor, Pepsi is a major competitor to Coca-Cola and is sold worldwide. Beyond its flagship cola, PepsiCo offers a variety of beverage options, including Diet Pepsi, Pepsi Max, and various fruit-flavored sodas, as well as non-cola beverages like Mountain Dew and Tropicana. Pepsi is also well-known for its impactful marketing campaigns, celebrity endorsements, and sponsorships of major events and sports leagues.
